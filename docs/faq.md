# General Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## What is the goal of this website?

The goal of the repository is to be high quality documention for users 
on how to find and use the national NAISS resources,
where 'high quality' is defined as (1) checked by scripts
(such as spellchecking), and (2) having guides that are
suitable as teaching material.
